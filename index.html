Our Story

Founded in 2009 in Khobar, Saudi Arabia, Marksys specializes in corporate gifts, promotional apparel, and custom-branded merchandise, partnering with top brands like Mont Blanc, Waterman, and Cross. Recognizing market needs, we expanded into professional workwear and flame-resistant PPE, serving oil & gas giants like Saudi Aramco. Later, we grew into uniform solutions for industries like healthcare, logistics, and hospitality. As an exclusive distributor of Tootal Fabrics (Holland) and global brands like 3M and Honeywell, we supply high-quality fabrics and safety gear, ensuring premium service across the Middle East. .
